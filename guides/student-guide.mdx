## Welcome, Student!

This guide will help you navigate the SWL Library Management System as a student user. Learn how to request equipment, borrow books, and manage your loans effectively.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Go to the registration page at `/auth/register`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Your Information">
    - Full name
    - Document ID (this will be your username)
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Select role: **Cliente** (Student)
    - Enter your program name (e.g., "Computer Science", "Engineering")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Your Password">
    Choose a secure password and confirm it
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit and Login">
    After successful registration, you'll be redirected to the login page
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Login Page">
    Navigate to `/auth/login`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    - Document ID (the one you registered with)
    - Password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    After login, you'll be redirected to your personal dashboard at `/dashboard`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Bookmark your dashboard page for quick access to all your loans and requests!
</Tip>

## Requesting Equipment

### Requesting a Laptop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Laptop Request">
    From your dashboard, click on "Request Laptop" or go to `/request/laptop`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a Laptop">
    Choose from the available laptops in the catalog. You'll see:
    - Laptop model and brand
    - Number of units available
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Environment">
    Select where you'll use the laptop:
    - **Interno**: Inside the library
    - **Externo**: Outside the library (take home)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Click submit to send your request to the librarian for approval
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Important Restrictions:**
- Students can only request **one laptop at a time**
- You cannot request another laptop while you have one pending, active, or overdue
- Wait for approval before picking up your equipment
</Warning>

### Requesting Accessories

As a student, you can request general accessories like headphones, chargers, and other non-computing equipment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Accessory Request">
    Navigate to `/request/accessory` from your dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Accessory">
    Choose from available general accessories (computing equipment is reserved for premium users)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Specify Quantity">
    Enter how many units you need (up to your limit)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Send your request for librarian approval
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Students can request up to **2 accessories simultaneously**. This limit helps ensure fair access for all users.
</Info>

## Requesting Books

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Book Catalog">
    Navigate to `/request/book` to see available books
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a Book">
    Choose from the catalog showing:
    - Book title
    - Author
    - Available copies
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Click to request the book
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Visit the Library Desk">
    After your request is approved, go to the library desk to pick up your book
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Book loans are automatically set with a **15-day due date** from approval. Plan accordingly!
</Note>

## Managing Your Loans

### Checking Loan Status

Your dashboard (`/dashboard`) displays all your loans with the following statuses:

- **Pendiente** (Pending): Awaiting librarian approval
- **Activo** (Active): Approved and in your possession
- **Atrasado** (Overdue): Past the due date - return immediately!
- **Devuelto** (Returned): Successfully returned
- **Rechazado** (Rejected): Request was denied

### Understanding Due Dates

Each loan has a due date displayed in your dashboard:

- **Equipment loans**: Typically 15 days from approval
- **Book loans**: 15 days from approval

The system automatically converts UTC times to Colombia time (UTC-5) for display.

<Warning>
**Late Returns:**
Returning items late may result in penalties. Books accrue a daily fine after the due date.
</Warning>

### Penalties for Late Books

The system automatically calculates penalties for overdue books:

- Penalty starts accumulating the day after the due date
- Default rate: $5,000 COP per day
- Penalty is frozen and recorded when you return the book
- No penalties for non-book items

<Tip>
Check your dashboard regularly to avoid late fees. Set reminders a day before your due dates!
</Tip>

## Profile Management

Access your profile at `/profile` to:

- View your personal information
- See your complete loan history
- Check your program affiliation
- Review contact details

<Info>
To update your profile information (email, phone, program), contact a system administrator.
</Info>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Plan Ahead">
    Request equipment a day before you need it to allow time for librarian approval during business hours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Return on Time">
    Set calendar reminders 1-2 days before due dates. Early returns are always appreciated!
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check Equipment Condition">
    When picking up laptops or accessories, verify the condition with the librarian. Report any issues immediately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Understand Your Limits">
    Remember your restrictions:
    - 1 laptop maximum
    - 2 accessories maximum
    - No access to specialized computing equipment
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Keep Contact Info Updated">
    Ensure your email and phone are current so librarians can reach you about your requests.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why was my laptop request rejected?">
    Common reasons:
    - You already have an active laptop loan
    - The item became unavailable
    - You have overdue items
    
    Check the observation field in your dashboard for the specific reason.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I request multiple books at once?">
    The current system processes one book request at a time. Submit separate requests for multiple books.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I need the item longer than 15 days?">
    Contact the library desk before your due date to request an extension. Extensions are granted case-by-case.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I return items?">
    Bring items to the library desk during operating hours. The librarian will process the return in the system and clear your loan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade to a premium account?">
    Yes! Contact a system administrator to upgrade your account to premium status for access to specialized equipment and higher limits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
